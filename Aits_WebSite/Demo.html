WELCOME TO AITS RAJAMPET AUTONOMOUS


Annamacharya Institute of Technology & Sciences, Rajampet is an AUTONOMOUS institution and is affiliated to JNTU, Anantapur.
The Institute was started in the year 1998 under the auspices of Annamacharya Educational Trust in picturesque surrounding
on a spacious 30 acre campus near Tallapaka, the birth place of Annamacharya, the renowned saint poet.
